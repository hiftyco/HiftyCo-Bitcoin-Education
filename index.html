What is Money?

This is the most important question of our time. The answer will change how you see the world.

💸

Fiat Money

Paper currency backed by... nothing. Governments print it endlessly.

  • Infinite supply
  • Loses ~3-7% value yearly
  • Can be frozen/seized
  • Requires trusted third parties
  • 24/7 printing presses
₿

Bitcoin

Digital gold. Money without masters. Scarce, secure, sovereign.

  • Fixed 21M supply
  • Deflationary by design
  • Censorship resistant
  • No middlemen needed
  • Halves every 4 years

5 Properties of Sound Money

What makes money... money?

🎯 1. Scarcity

Bitcoin's supply is mathematically fixed at 21 million. No central bank can print more. Ever. This is its most important property.

🛡️ 2. Censorship Resistance

No government can freeze your funds. No bank can block transactions. True financial sovereignty.

🌍 3. Borderless

Send $1 or $1 billion anywhere in the world in minutes. No middlemen. No borders. No permission needed.

⚡ 4. Divisibility

One BTC = 100,000,000 satoshis (sats). Buy $5 worth. No amount is too small.

🔐 5. Self-Custody

Be your own bank. Your keys, your coins. Not your keys, not your coins. Hold your wealth yourself.
