📚 Myths vs Facts

Separating Bitcoin facts from FUD

❌ MYTH: Bitcoin is used for illegal activities
✅ FACT: Cash is still #1 for illegal use. Bitcoin is MORE traceable than cash.
❌ MYTH: Bitcoin wastes energy
✅ FACT: Mining uses excess energy and incentivizes renewables. Most secure network on Earth.
❌ MYTH: Bitcoin will be replaced by "better" crypto
✅ FACT: Strongest network effect & decentralization. #1 since 2009.
❌ MYTH: Bitcoin has no intrinsic value
✅ FACT: Scarcity, durability, divisibility, portability, verifiability. Same as gold, but digital.
