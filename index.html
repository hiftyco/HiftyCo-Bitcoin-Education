The Story of Money

For 5,000 years, humans used gold as money. It was scarce, durable, and divisible.

In 1971, that changed. Governments could now print unlimited money. Your savings began to lose value every year.

Then in 2009, someone created Bitcoin. A new form of money that can't be printed. Ever.
