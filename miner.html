Learn About Bitcoin Mining

🔐

What is Mining?

Bitcoin mining is the process of adding transaction records to Bitcoin's public ledger. Miners compete to solve complex mathematical puzzles, and the winner gets newly created Bitcoin as a reward.

⚡

Hash Rate

Hash rate measures the miner's computational power. Higher hash rate = more chances to find blocks. The Bitaxe Ultra produces around 450 GH/s (billion hashes per second).

🏊

Mining Pools

Pools combine many miners' hash power to find blocks more frequently. Rewards are shared proportionally based on each miner's contribution.

🎯

Proof of Work

Bitcoin uses Proof of Work (PoW) consensus. Miners must prove they've expended computational energy. This secures the network and creates new coins fairly.

📉

Halving

Every ~4 years, Bitcoin block rewards halve. In 2024, rewards went from 6.25 to 3.125 BTC. This decreasing supply is what makes Bitcoin deflationary.

💰

Profitability

Mining profit depends on: electricity cost, Bitcoin price, network difficulty, and hashrate. Use the calculator below to estimate your potential earnings.
